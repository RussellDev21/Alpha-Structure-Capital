THE FIRM

Institutional discipline. Entrepreneurial focus.

Alpha Structure Capital is being developed around a focused investment philosophy: seek repeatable edges in market structure, insist on defined catalysts, and evaluate downside before upside.

Firm overview

The firm's operating model is intended to combine focused internal decision-making with independent, institutional-quality administration, audit, legal, compliance, custody, and reporting functions.

The investment philosophy is deliberately narrow: analyze structure before expected return, identify the catalyst and time horizon, underwrite adverse outcomes, emphasize liquidity and disciplined position sizing, and seek repeatable, differentiated sources of return.

Governance principles

Clear investment, trading, cash, and approval authorities
Independent administration and reconciliation
Documented valuation and expense-allocation policies
Compliance monitoring and recordkeeping
Business-continuity and key-person planning
Secure investor reporting

Alignment with investors

Alignment is intended to come from structure rather than slogans: performance-based compensation, clearly documented responsibilities, an operating model intended to incorporate independent administration, reconciliation and valuation oversight, and reporting that lets investors see what they own and why.

Specific fee, liquidity, and governance terms will be set out exclusively in the definitive offering documents of the applicable vehicle.

Institutional operating model

The firm intends to engage independent legal, administrative, audit, tax, custody and compliance providers appropriate to the final fund structure. Provider information will be disclosed after appointments are completed and approved for publication.

Business continuity and cybersecurity

The operating framework is intended to include documented business-continuity planning, secure systems and access controls, incident-response procedures, and periodic review of service-provider security practices. No control environment eliminates operational or cyber risk entirely.
