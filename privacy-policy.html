Privacy Policy

Information collected

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, organization, jurisdiction, investor type, approximate investment range, and the contents of your messages, along with limited technical information generated by your visit.

Investor inquiries

Information submitted through investor-inquiry forms is used to evaluate and respond to your request, to conduct eligibility review, and to maintain required records. It is not used for unrelated marketing.

Accreditation and eligibility information

Eligibility review may involve additional information collected through secure channels. We do not request accreditation evidence, government identifiers, or financial account details through public website forms.

AML/KYC information

If you proceed toward an investment, anti-money-laundering and know-your-customer information will be collected through secure, authenticated processes and handled in accordance with applicable law.

Cookies and analytics

The website may use limited cookies or privacy-conscious analytics to understand aggregate usage. We do not use session-replay tools on inquiry forms and do not sell personal information.

Service providers

We may share information with service providers who support our operations—such as hosting, form processing, administration, and compliance—under obligations of confidentiality and limited use.

Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed for the purposes described here, to comply with legal and regulatory obligations, and to maintain required books and records.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information, or to object to certain processing. Requests can be made through the Contact page.

Cross-border visitors

If you access this website from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States, where data-protection laws may differ from those of your jurisdiction.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Policy updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy or requests regarding your information may be submitted through the Contact page.
