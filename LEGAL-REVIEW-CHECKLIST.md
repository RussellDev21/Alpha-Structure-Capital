# Legal Review Checklist

Items requiring review and approval by U.S. securities counsel (and, where
noted, privacy counsel) before the website is published.

## Whole-site

- [ ] All public copy reviewed against Rule 506(c) general-solicitation rules
- [ ] Confirmation that no statement promises or implies guaranteed returns,
      capital protection, safety, low correlation, or tax efficiency
- [ ] Announcement-bar eligibility language
- [ ] Footer disclaimers (three paragraphs, all pages)
- [ ] "Being developed" / forward-looking phrasing — confirm accuracy at launch

## Page-specific

- [ ] disclosures.html — entire page (marked DRAFT in code comment), including
      the social-media / general-solicitation disclosure and the
      no-governmental-approval statement
- [ ] privacy-policy.html — entire page; confirm which privacy laws apply and
      whether specific compliance statements (e.g. GDPR/CCPA) may be made
- [ ] terms-of-use.html — entire page; supply governing jurisdiction
- [ ] investment-process.html — hypothetical educational illustration
      (labels, framing, and figures)
- [ ] strategy.html — yield-to-worst definition and SPAC/trust descriptions
- [ ] risk-management.html — material-risk list vs. offering documents
- [ ] investor-access.html — access-process description, FAQ answers
      (especially accreditation verification and foreign investors),
      consent language, and form fields collected
- [ ] articles/ — all three educational articles
- [ ] contact.html — inquiry categories and consent language

## Process confirmations

- [ ] Verification procedure for accredited-investor status (506(c))
- [ ] Qualified-client / qualified-purchaser screening, where applicable
- [ ] Form-data handling, retention period, and access controls
- [ ] Jurisdictional access restrictions, if any
- [ ] Recordkeeping obligations for website content and form submissions
- [ ] Social-media policy consistent with the site's disclosure
- [ ] Sign-off procedure for any future performance content (none exists today)
